ABOUT

         VIT Bhopal University, located on the Bhopal-Indore highway, an arterial road connecting the two most beautiful and cleanest cities of India,
         is a place where knowledge meets motivation. Spread in an area of 200 acres, in the lap of fresh fauna and flora, the campus boasts two playgrounds
         with a large pond adding to its scenic beauty.

        VIT Bhopal was established in 2017 through an act passed by the Government of Madhya Pradesh. The campus of the university is spread over an area
         of 200 acres of land. VIT University Bhopal is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The university is also
        recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC). There are 6 schools on Engineering and Management, functioning under VIT Bhopal.
         They are the School of Advanced Sciences and Languages, School of Bioengineering, School of Computing Science and Engineering, School of
         Electrical and Electronics Engineering, School of Mechanical Engineering and VIT Business School
